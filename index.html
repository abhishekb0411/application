Our Vision

At Shu Ra POWER, we are committed to delivering clean and green energy solutions for EV charging. Our innovative approach combines the power of Vertical Axis Wind Turbines and Solar Energy to provide sustainable, eco-friendly charging stations.

Vertical Axis Wind Turbines

Harnessing the power of the wind, our Vertical Axis Wind Turbines offer efficient energy generation in a compact design. These turbines are designed to work in urban and rural environments, capturing wind energy from all directions to power our EV charging stations.

Solar Power

Our clean and green charging stations use photovoltaic panels to capture solar energy. This clean, renewable source of energy combined with VAWT, ensures a constant and reliable power supply for electric vehicles.

Environmental Benefits

Shu Ra POWER reduces the carbon footprint of EV charging by utilizing 100% renewable energy. This means less reliance on fossil fuels and a significant reduction in greenhouse gas emissions.

Innovative Technology

Our dual approach—combining wind and solar energy—ensures that energy is harvested in various weather conditions. This maximizes energy efficiency and provides a reliable power source, no matter the time of day or location.
